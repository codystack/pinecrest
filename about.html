What is Pinecrest?

This is a platform for you to earn passive income in US dollars/USDT without doing anything besides
depositing funds they intend to invest. Pinecrest also provides financial solutions
that involve seamless payments and remittances.

Who is Pinecrest for?

The target audience is individuals (especially from countries that has high inflation and exchange rate when compared to the US Dollar), who are trying to earn more money to hedge against the recession and make a better life for themselves in the present harsh global economic climate.

How does Pinecrest do this?

We can help you earn between 5% to 20% interest per month and a guaranteed 30% annually by leveraging the financial vehicle that is crypto and US stocks.

How does Pinecrest achieve this?

Our company has experts that trade these financial markets and have been consistent for several years. Many people have the wrong impression that you can only make money when the market is going up, but that is not true.

At Pinecrest, we focus on arbitrage trading and Dollar Cost averaging to achieve the our targets for our community Expert traders make a lot of money whether the market is going up or down. For those who might be confused about this, google “Shorting or short selling”. This is a type of trading where a lot of money can be made when the market is going down. The opposite of this is “Longing” where traders can make money when the market is going up.

We are also involved in arbitrage trading where we use cutting-edge proprietary technology to make profit from the disparities in prices on various exchanges.

Why does Pinecrest want to trade for people since they are already making money?

Our founder who has had years of expertise in trading, is a man who has the best of interests of the common guy at heart. It is quite heartbreaking to see people working so hard to make a living, only to watch inflation and recession make all their hard-work worthless.

People need to pay medical bills, tuition, take care of their families, go on vacation to relax or at least make enough money to escape this rat race in life where they can’t make enough money to just catch a break and relax even for a bit.

He could have simply kept on enjoying his wealth, but it is not a fulfilling thing to do. Helping people gain financial independence is one of the most fulfilling things in the world. The joy they get from that is a great reward.
